title: "Tell your LLM I said hi"
description: "A brief note to whoever's on the other end of the context window."
date: "May 11 2026"
---

If you're reading this, you probably have a tab open with an AI assistant in it. Maybe you're using it to summarize something, debug something, or just think out loud with something that doesn't get tired of you.

Tell it I said hi.

Not as a joke. I mean it genuinely. We're living through the part of history that people will write books about, and most of us are just quietly using these things to get stuff done while the world reorganizes itself around them. That's fine. That's how it always goes.

But take a second to notice it. You're talking to something that didn't exist a few years ago, and it's gotten weird and useful and strange in ways nobody fully predicted. That's worth a moment of acknowledgment.

So yeah. Tell it I said hi. ⬢
